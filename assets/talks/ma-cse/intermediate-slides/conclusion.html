To summarise

Mechanics Academy aims to be a valuable resource for anyone interested in learning mechanics

In particular, it intends to:

  • Serve as a device to attract new learners to a rich and useful topic
  • Act as a catalogue of high-quality learning resources for mechanics
  • Provide a community of co-learners and mechanics experts to approach for help while learning
  • Contain mechanisms to test learning and gauge competency
